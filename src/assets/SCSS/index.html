Lidiflu SmartDelivery Project

Welcome to your online portal

SmartDelivery is part of how we are working differently with our clients – combining innovative resourcing and processes with our award winning technology.

SmartDelivery can help achieve benefits for you, including:

  • managing your legal workflow, including smooth running of major projects across portfolios
  • freeing up your internal legal team resource and making them more efficient
  • reducing your exposure to risk by harnessing management information, allowing you to add real value

Guidance

Before navigating the site for the first time, we recommend that you visit our online tutorial “Navigating your SmartDelivery platform” [Link here]. This will help you understand the full functionality of the portal available to you.

As a quick guide: To access project specific information and set your own filters, please click on the Project Name in the toolbar above.

To run pre-populated reports, please click on the Reports option in the toolbar above.

Clicking SmartDelivery will bring you back to this homepage.

The search function allows you to search the site, including by connection ID or Loan ID.

If you have any questions about the portal or are having difficulty navigating the site, please contact Jen Marshall jennifer.marshall@yolo.com.
